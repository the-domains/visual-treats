Saatchi Art Artist: Konrad Pitala; Color 2012 Photography "*tri*angle* #1"

Part of a triptych. Photographic artwork depicting the power of light, printed on metallic base paper (Epson HDR archival grade), mounted on a foam. Created by exposing the film in a light-leaking camera (Lomography Diana F+), then scanned in hi resolution & digitally added graphic motive - triangles.
